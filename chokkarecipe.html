Litti Chokha

Description: Traditional Bihari dish made with roasted wheat balls and mashed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Sattu (roasted gram flour)
  • Spices
  • Vegetables for chokha (eggplant, tomato, potato)

Instructions

Prepare litti balls stuffed with sattu, roast until golden, and serve with chokha made from mashed vegetables and spices.
