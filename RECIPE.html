Chocolate Cake

Description: A rich and moist chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 baking powder
  • 1 cup baking soda
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract

Instructions

To make a chocolate cake, simply mix the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) in a bowl, then whisk the wet ingredients (eggs or condensed milk, oil or butter, milk, vanilla) separately. Combine them into a smooth batter, pour into a greased tin, and bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 30 minutes until set. Let it cool, then finish with frosting or ganache for a delicious treat.
